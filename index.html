Get Know About Us!

Jijiwisha Society (Reg. No. LUC/00699/2018-2019) works in women and child welfare, environment, mental wellness, health and hygiene, legal awareness, living conditions, and self-defense training in government institutions.

Jijiwisha society is inspired by the doctrine of Welfare state which talks of maximum happiness for maximum people that can be achieved by adopting all the possible ways of spreading happiness.

Our Feature

Why you trust us?

Jijiwisha Society is a dynamic organization dedicated to social welfare and community development. Engaging in extensive social work, the society emphasizes environmental sustainability through large-scale tree plantations, having planted 10,500 saplings across various regions. They collaborate on women and child safety initiatives, offering PoSH training and POCSO awareness, with over 60,000 people trained and active participation in major Internal Committees for gender sensitization.

Testimonial

Our Testimonial!!

I know Jijiwisha society for over two years now, and the team Jijiwisha is truly justifying its name under the able leadership of Ms. Manjary. They are not just supporting society environmentally by actively doing plantation and maintenance of plants but they also ensure the reduced mortality of plants considerably. This way I feel the money donated to the cause is being used properly. Secondly, Jijiwisha is also supporting society economically by involving rural women to make decorated diyas during Diwali season and papads during holi and earn something while being at home. I feel proud to support Jijiwisha society. They are living their name in letter and spirit.

Dr Amit Sinha
Senior consultant

I have been associated with Jijiwisha society through Facebook Twitter and WhatsApp. I have observed that the society is doing missionary work without any monetary overtones in the field of Environmental Conservation work and Community awareness. The society is devoted to the human cause. Their work in greening the earth is commendable. The group has taken tremendous effort in training and educating the masses on environmental conservation and other social issues. I am sure the work of the Jijiwisha Society would be widely acknowledged in the future.

Mr. K. P. Singh
Additional Principal Chief Conservator OF Forests, Madhya Pradesh (Monitoring & Evaluation)

I had the privilege to be associated with its inception and truly love the work and efforts being put in by Manjary’s team - more power and prosperity to those associated to bring in new minds to join their cause.
